Call for Papers

We seek to provide talks encompassing a wide variety of technologies. We welcome everyone with a strong interest in functional programming for the web to share their knowledge and experience.

Whether it is a topic you have been researching for a while, or a piece of technology that has been giving you a hard time until you finally managed to reveal its inner workings, we would really like to hear what you have to share!

About Sofia

What better time to enjoy the first days of autumn than late September? And what better place than sunny Sofia?

We have delicious food, affordable prices, and a rapidly growing programming community with members involved in a variety of technologies.

There are a myriad of activities you can partake in outside of our humble conference: go for a hike in Vitosha mountain, plan a one-day trip to Rila Monastery, or simply enjoy a relaxed walk on the streets of Sofia, taking a nice meal at one of the many restaurants in the city center.
